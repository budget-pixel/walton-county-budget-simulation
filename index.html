Trend

Property Tax Revenue and Supported Expenses

Projected revenues are based on taxable property values and the proposed millage rate. The FY2028 forecast assumes approximately $1.67 billion in reduced taxable value, while FY2029 assumes an additional $1.28 billion reduction in taxable value, based on estimates provided by the Walton County Property Appraiser's Office. These reductions decrease the property tax revenue available to support county services.

Forecast

Projected Revenue Shortfall

Direct revenue reductions reflect the estimated Property Tax revenue loss from the proposed homestead exemption increase. The structural budget gap represents the expenditures that would need to be adjusted as reduced revenue limits the funding available for county services in future years.
